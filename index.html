01. About Me

I'm a Background Concept Designer specializing in building rich, atmospheric environments for interactive and cinematic media. From sweeping fantasy landscapes to gritty cyberpunk alleyways, I design worlds that players and viewers want to step into.

With a keen eye for composition, lighting, and narrative design, I transform rough ideas into fully realized visual worlds. I work across multiple styles — from painterly realism to stylized art directions — always driven by story and emotion.
